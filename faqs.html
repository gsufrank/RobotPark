Frequently Asked Questions


General

The Robotic Playground is a place where students from all over the world can come and explore the wonders of robotics programming!

This is a place intended for students primarily, but anyone with an interest in basic programming, or robotics are welcome.

Anyone who desires to interact with the playground are welcome to schedule an appointment. Once you have selected your time and date, you will receieve a link to a special page designed just for you! This is a secret link so do not share with anyone. The link will not become active until you 5 minutes prior to your scheduled time

Once you have accessed the secret page, you can control the robots by writing your own scripts (following the guidelines set before you) or by uisng the plot points to control the robots around the 5 x 5 grid.

Scheduling

Click on the 'Schedule' link and follow the steps. You will select an available date and an available time. Then a secret link will be sent to you. Do not share the link with anyone!

A session will last for 10 minutes. We do this to keep our video recording processing down. This allows for us to allow as many people to access the site as possible.

Unfortunately, you will need to go through the process of scheduling a new date and time in order to be fair to other users.

Software

We use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and PHP.

This is pending as of this moment. The idea is that they will use C++ and our site will use a python interpreter for users to control them.

Video

Once your session has ended, allow for 10 minutes to pass and then check the archive page. At this point the video should be added to the end of the archive carousel.

At this time, we do not have the ability for you to download the videos. This will be added in the future.

The videos will stay up forever at this point in time. It is possible we will need to add an auto-removal system in the future.
